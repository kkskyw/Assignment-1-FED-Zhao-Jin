Awards Received

MAMA Award for Song of the Year 2023- Ditto

The MAMA Award for Song of the Year 2023 was awarded to "Ditto" by NewJeans, solidifying the group’s status as a major force in the K-pop industry. Released on December 19, 2022, as part of their "OMG" single album, "Ditto" quickly became a cultural phenomenon, praised for its innovative music production, nostalgic storytelling, and emotionally resonant lyrics.

Japan Record Award for Excellence Work 2024- Supernatural

The Japan Record Award for Excellence Work 2024 was awarded to "Supernatural" by NewJeans, celebrating the group’s continuous rise as global music icons. Released as a part of their recent album, "Supernatural" showcased the group's unique artistry, innovative sound, and storytelling prowess, earning widespread acclaim both in Japan and internationally.

MAMA Award for Best Female Group 2023

The MAMA Award for Best Female Group 2023 was awarded to NewJeans, recognizing the group's extraordinary achievements and their profound impact on the global music scene. This award celebrated their innovation, artistry, and the way they have reshaped the landscape of K-pop since their debut.
