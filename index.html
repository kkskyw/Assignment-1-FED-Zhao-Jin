Introduction

NewJeans is a 5-member girl group formed by ADOR, a sub-label of HYBE. The group debuted on July 22, 2022. The group is composed of five members: Minji, Hanni, Danielle, Haerin, and Hyein. Their debut album "New Jeans" was a commercial success, earning them the title of 2022's 'It Girl' group.

Albums Collection

2022

NewJeans debuted in 2022 with their self-titled EP, New Jeans. The EP featured four tracks: "Attention", "Hype Boy", "Cookie", and "Hurt".

2023

In 2023, NewJeans continued their success with the single album OMG, which included the tracks "OMG" and "Ditto". While OMG presented an upbeat vibe, Ditto sparked fan discussions and theories, further elevating the group's reputation.

2024

In 2024, NewJeans released their first full-length album, Supernatural. This album received critical acclaim, cementing their status as global icons and trailblazers in the K-pop industry.
